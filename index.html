Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a full setup guide for new users?

+

Yes. We ship a full onboarding and operations guide that covers install, license activation, LM Studio setup, task configuration, and troubleshooting. Open Getting Started for the complete walkthrough.

Do I need to keep my computer on?

+

Yes, Nexus runs on your local machine as a desktop application. Your computer needs to stay on for continuous applications. However, you can set scheduled automation windows so it only runs during specific times. This also keeps your data completely private—everything stays on your device.

What AI models does it support?

+

Nexus is LLM-agnostic. You can plug in your preferred model: LM Studio (local), OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic Claude, or Ollama. This flexibility means you control which AI powers your applications and can choose based on cost and privacy preferences.

Is my data sent anywhere?

+

No. Everything runs locally on your machine by default. Your resume, job preferences, and application history never leave your computer. The only communication is a periodic license verification ping. If you use an external LLM (OpenAI, Claude), only the job description gets sent to process—your personal data remains private.

What platforms are supported?

+

Nexus currently supports LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor. It also integrates with external ATS systems like Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and BambooHR. We're continuously adding platform support based on user demand.

When will Nexus be available?

+

We're currently in closed beta. Join the waitlist to get early access as soon as Nexus becomes available. Early access participants will get special pricing and priority support.
