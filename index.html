FAQ

Questions users actually ask before they trust a local operator.

Is there a complete setup guide for new users?

+

Yes. The setup guide covers install, license activation, AI runtime setup, job-site sign-in, search configuration, and troubleshooting.

Do I need to keep my computer on while Nexus runs?

+

Yes. Nexus is a desktop operator that runs locally on your machine. That is also why your resumes, history, and configuration stay private by default.

What models can I use with Nexus?

+

Nexus can use the built-in runtime or connect to LM Studio, Ollama, OpenAI, and Anthropic. The guide includes the exact setup flow for each option.

Does my data leave my computer?

+

By default, no. Nexus runs locally and keeps your resumes, preferences, and application history on your device. If you choose an external model provider, only the job description is sent for processing.

How do beta users download the app?

+

Approved users can sign in with Discord on this page to unlock the download, or manage their key directly through the license portal.
