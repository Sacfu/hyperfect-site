Frequently Asked Questions

Is this against LinkedIn's Terms of Service?

+

Nexus operates in a gray area. It automates your own profile's actions (using browser automation), similar to how you would manually apply. However, we recommend reviewing LinkedIn's ToS and using Nexus responsibly. It's designed to augment your job search, not violate platform policies. Use at your own discretion.

Do I need to keep my computer on?

+

Yes, Nexus runs on your local machine as a desktop application. Your computer needs to stay on for continuous applications. However, you can set scheduled automation windows so it only runs during specific times. This also keeps your data completely private‚Äîeverything stays on your device.

What AI/LLM does it use?

+

Nexus is LLM-agnostic. You can plug in your preferred model: LM Studio (local), OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude), or Ollama. This flexibility means you control which AI powers your applications and can choose based on cost and privacy preferences.

Is my data sent anywhere?

+

No. Everything runs locally on your machine by default. Your resume, job preferences, and application history never leave your computer. The only communication is a periodic license verification ping. If you use an external LLM (OpenAI, Claude), only the job description gets sent to process‚Äîyour personal data remains private.

What platforms are supported?

+

Nexus currently supports LinkedIn Easy Apply, Indeed, and Glassdoor. It also integrates with external ATS systems like Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and BambooHR. We're continuously adding platform support based on user demand.

Can I get a refund?

+

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If Nexus doesn't meet your expectations within the first week, we'll refund your subscription in full‚Äîno questions asked. We're confident you'll love it.
